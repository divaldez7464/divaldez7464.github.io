Here's a time line of Stephen Curry's life:

  • 1988- Born in Akron, Ohio, U.S
  • 2006- Played college basketball for the Davison Wildcats
  • 2009- Curry was selected by the Warriors with the seventh overall pick in the 2009 NBA draft
  • 2013- Curry set the NBA record for three-pointers made in a regular season, with 272
  • 2015- Curry won his first league MVP award and led the Warriors to their first championship since 1975
  • 2017- Won NBA Finals against the Cleveland Cavaliers
  • 2018- Won back-to-back NBA Finals against the Cleveland Cavaliers
  • 2022- Won NBA Finals against the Boston Celtics
  • Present- Still plays for the Golden State Worriors
